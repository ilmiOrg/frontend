Frequently Asked Questions

How does the university matching work?
Our AI analyzes your academic profile, preferences, financial situation, and career goals to find universities that are the best fit for you. We consider factors like program offerings, admission requirements, costs, and success rates.
Is UniversityMatch free to use?
Yes! We offer a free basic plan that includes university matching and limited scholarship search. We also have premium plans with advanced features for students who want more comprehensive support.
How accurate are the university matches?
Our matching algorithm has a 95% success rate, meaning 95% of students who follow our recommendations get accepted to at least one of their matched universities.
Can I get help with my applications?
Yes! Our premium plans include application tracking, expert consultations, and personalized guidance throughout the entire application process.
